Our Story

A Love Written in Gold

It started at a gallery opening in Maboneng. Marieka was captivated by the art, Michael was captivated by Marieka. A coffee turned into brunch, turned into an all-day adventure.

Two years of rooftop sunsets, road trips to the Drakensberg, and midnight conversations later, Michael proposed under the stars overlooking the Joburg skyline.

“Whatever our souls are made of, his and mine are the same.”
